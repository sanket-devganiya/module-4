What is Normalization?


======================================================ANS===================================================================

 Normalization refers to the process of organizing data in a database to reduce redundancy and improve data integrity. 
 It typically involves dividing a database into smaller, related tables and defining relationships between them to ensure
 that data is stored efficiently. The goal is to eliminate duplicate data and ensure that updates, insertions, and deletions
 of data are performed without introducing inconsistencies.
